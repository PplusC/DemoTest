Novak Djokovic Advances to Semifinals After Epic Five-Set Match

By Sarah Johnson April 21, 2025 6 min read

Novak Djokovic celebrates after clinching victory in a grueling five-set battle.

In a display of sheer determination, Novak Djokovic battled back from two sets down to defeat rising star Carlos Alcaraz in a thrilling five-set quarterfinal match.

A Rollercoaster Battle

The match lasted over four hours, with both players pushing each other to the limit. Alcaraz took the first two sets with incredible shot-making, but Djokovic’s resilience and experience came through as he clawed his way back.

Turning Point

Midway through the third set, Djokovic broke serve after an intense 18-shot rally, shifting the momentum. From that point on, the Serbian star was relentless, dominating the fourth set and holding his nerve in the final set tiebreak.

Chasing History

This victory advances Djokovic to his 47th career Grand Slam semifinal, further extending his all-time record. A win here could bring him yet another major title, adding to his legacy as one of the greatest to ever play the game.
