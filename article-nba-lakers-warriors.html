LeBron Leads Lakers to Thrilling Overtime Victory Against Warriors

By Michael Johnson April 20, 2025 6 min read

LeBron James celebrates after hitting the game-winning shot in overtime.

In a nail-biting matchup at the Crypto.com Arena, the Los Angeles Lakers secured a dramatic overtime victory against the Golden State Warriors, with LeBron James leading the charge with 38 points.

A Battle of Titans

Both teams traded leads throughout the game, showcasing elite defense and highlight-reel plays. Stephen Curry dazzled with 33 points, including 7 three-pointers, while Anthony Davis added 22 points and 15 rebounds for the Lakers.

The Game-Winning Moment

With the game tied at 128 in overtime, LeBron took matters into his own hands. Driving past Draymond Green, he finished with a signature fadeaway jumper that gave the Lakers a two-point lead with 1.4 seconds remaining.

The Warriors' final attempt fell short as Klay Thompson's buzzer-beater rimmed out, sealing a vital win for the Lakers in their push for playoff seeding.

Playoff Implications

This victory moves the Lakers up to the 5th seed in the Western Conference, while the Warriors now face a tougher path with only four games left in the regular season.
